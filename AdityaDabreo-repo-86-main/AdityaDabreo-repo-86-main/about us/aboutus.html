About Ettarra Coffee House

At Ettarra Coffee House, we are more than just a coffee shop; we are a destination where creative minds and culinary excellence meet. Known for our eclectic ambiance and themed supper club events, we offer our patrons an unparalleled experience that blends fine dining, themed atmospheres, and memorable community engagement.

As we expand into hosting exclusive supper club events across various themed rooms, our goal remains the same: to provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for our customers, from booking to check-in, and everything in between.

Our Vision

We believe in transforming dining experiences by merging culture, creativity, and community into each event we host. Whether it’s an intimate dinner, a themed night, or a grand event, Ettarra Coffee House is committed to making each occasion a lasting memory.

Our Platform

To meet the growing demand for our unique supper club experiences, we have developed a state-of-the-art platform that streamlines event management and customer interaction. This platform allows patrons to easily discover and book seats for themed events, while our team benefits from advanced event planning, inventory control, and real-time booking updates.

Why Choose Ettarra?

  • Diverse Themed Experiences: Each event offers a new and unique atmosphere, creating memorable culinary adventures.
  • Easy Bookings: Our visual seat selection and booking system makes finding the perfect spot effortless.
  • Effortless Check-Ins: With QR code-based entry, your experience starts the moment you arrive.
  • AI-Powered Recommendations: Personalized event suggestions ensure that every event matches your tastes and preferences.
  • Customer-Centric: Post-event reviews, feedback, and a waiting list feature ensure that you never miss an opportunity to attend our most popular events.

Join us at Ettarra Coffee House and be part of a growing community where every supper club is a journey, and every meal is a new experience.
